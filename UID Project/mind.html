MIND AND BODY CLASSES

ACROYOGA CLASS

AcroYoga elevates the connection between you and others through movement, connection and play. It is a bodyweight practice that combines Yoga, Acrobatics and healing arts.

ASHTANGA YOGA CLASS

A slightly more vigorous style of yoga; synchronise your breathing with a progressive series of postures, sun salutations, and poses.

BOXING YOGA CLASS

Fusing boxing technique and philosophy with Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga, BoxingYoga is an exciting and challenging yoga workout.

TAI CHI CLASSES

Helps lower stress and anxiety levels and increase energy. A series of slow, controlled movements and postures help build muscle strength, co-ordination, balance and flexibility

FITNESS PILATES CLASS

Fitness Pilates utilises all of the principals and includes many of the mat work exercises in traditional Pilates.

YOGA FIT CLASS

An upbeat yoga fit class concentrating on the transition from one posture to another.

FLEXIBILITY CLASSES

Improve flexibility, mobility and agility in this purposely designed class. Effective in reducing the risk of injury, improving posture and coordination.

HATHA YOGA CLASS

Hatha Yoga was originally intended to prep the body for deep meditation. Hatha Yoga encompasses almost all styles of modern yoga.

HOT PILATES CLASS

Hot Pilates is an alternative treatment for back pain which encompasses the basic principles of Pilates and Yoga but utilises a room temperature of 35 degrees or more.

MEDITATION CLASS

A half an hour guided meditation class. The meditation technique used is called 'Anapana' which means to be mindful of ones breath.

SOULBODY BARRE CLASS

A seamless flow that incorporates both dynamic and isometric movements using a Ballet Bar.

STRETCHING CLASS

Enjoy relaxing music while experiencing a total body stretch to leave you refreshed and restored in this quick pick-me-up program.
